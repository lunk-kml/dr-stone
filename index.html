Senku's Characteristics

  1. He's very straight forward.
  2. He's always radiated with confidence.
  3. He can sometime come off as arrogant.
  4. Most of the time, he always takes the logical approach.
  5. At times, he can be overconfident, but he never let it cloud his judgement.
  6. Everything he says is always backed up by his knowledge of science.
  7. He may sometimes be arrogant, but he never look down on those who are less knowledgeable than him.
